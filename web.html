Code-Tester is a beginner-friendly coding space built completely with HTML and CSS. It’s designed to show how powerful frontend coding can be, even without complex frameworks or backend systems. With Code-Tester, you get a clear and simple environment to learn, practice, and grow your web development skills. Our app lets you write and save code snippets, giving you a personal space to experiment with layouts, colors, and styles. Every line of code you add shapes the page instantly, helping you understand how structure (HTML) and design (CSS) work together to create beautiful websites. Whether you are just starting out or revising your basics, Code-Tester helps you practice frontend coding in a clean and simple interface, save your code and build your own collection of designs, experiment with styles and layouts to learn faster, and gain confidence by seeing your work take shape in real time. Code-Tester is not just a project – it’s a practice ground for creativity. From simple webpages to stylish designs, you can bring your ideas to life with just a few lines of code. Start building today and make your first step in web development with Code-Tester! 🌐💻
